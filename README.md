<h1>Face Recognition System</h1>
This project is a Face Recognition System built with Python, leveraging the power of <b>OpenCV</b> and <b>NumPy</b>. The system is capable of:

<li><b>Recognizing Human Faces:</b> Accurately detects and identifies individual faces.</li>
<li><b>Human Body Detection:</b> Identifies and tracks human bodies within a video or image.</li>
<li><b>Emotion Recognition:</b> Analyzes facial expressions to determine emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, and more.</li>
<h3>Features</h3>
<li><b>Real-time Processing:</b> Performs recognition and detection in real time using a camera feed.</li>
<li><b>Scalable:</b> Easily extendable to support additional functionalities or integrate with other systems.</li>
<li><b>Accurate and Efficient:</b> Optimized for high accuracy and performance.</li>
<h3>Technologies Used</h3>
<li><b>Python:</b> The core programming language for this project.</li>
<li><b>OpenCV:</b> For image and video processing, face, and body detection.</li>
<li><b>NumPy:</b> For numerical computations and data manipulation.</li>
<li><b>Pre-trained Models:</b> Utilizes pre-trained models for face, body, and emotion recognition.</li>
<h3>Use Cases</h3>
<li><b>Security Systems:</b> Identify and track individuals in restricted areas.</li>
<li><b>Smart Applications:</b> Enhance user experience in smart home or retail solutions.</li>
<li><b>Analytics:</b> Analyze human behavior based on body movements and emotions.</li>
